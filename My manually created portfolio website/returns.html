Return Policy

At Apeke Gold, we want you to be fully satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not satisfied, we offer a 30-day return policy for unused and undamaged items.

Return Conditions

To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it. It must also be in the original packaging.

Return Process

To initiate a return, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact us at Omowonuolakehinde1@gmail.com with your order number and reason for the return.
  2. Once approved, we will send you a return shipping label.
  3. Pack the item securely in its original packaging and attach the shipping label.
  4. Ship the item back to us.

Please note that you will be responsible for the return shipping costs unless the item was damaged or defective upon arrival.

Refunds

Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If your return is approved, your refund will be processed and a credit will be applied to your original payment method within 7-10 business days.

Exchanges

We only replace items if they are defective or damaged. If you need to exchange an item for the same item, please contact us at Omowonuolakehinde1@gmail.com.
